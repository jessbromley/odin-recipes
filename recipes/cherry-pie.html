Cherry Almond Pie

This delicious lasagna recipe will become your go-to for family game night!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Basil
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Cook beef until browned
  3. Combine browned beef with pasta sauce and basil and simmer for 10 minutes on Low/Med heat
  4. When pasta is done cooking, layer the bottom of baking pan with one sheet of pasta.
  5. Add an even layer of beef and sauce mixture on top of the pasta sheet.
  6. Add a layer of ricotta cheese on top of the beef mixture
  7. Add another pasta sheet
  8. Repeat steps 4 through 6 once more
  9. Bake for 20 minutes.
